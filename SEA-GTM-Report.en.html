🎯 Product Vision: From eKYC API to Professional Consultants

Why not just "locally deploy the eKYC API"?

There are many vendors around the world doing localization: Shufti Pro has local deployment options, GoAbacus Go1, Fourth Paradigm SageOne. They are all positioned as "all-in-one hardware" or "private cloud solutions" - essentially moving the eKYC API from the US cloud to the local cloud.

Our differences: ADVANCE.AI’s product is not a replacement API; Build an advisory system around API as the core . Banks purchase us not just to check compliance, but to get a An AI system that can understand business, provide suggestions, and assist decision-making 。

Four consulting function modules

1️⃣ Identity Risk Research and Judgment

core output Per-case reasoning, not just fractions
Typical applications ✓ Why is this person considered a high risk?
✓ What are the characteristics of this person compared to yesterday’s fraud case?
✓ What documents or biometrics should be reviewed next?
difference vs Jumio: a "consultant" not a "grading machine"

2️⃣ Compliance Consultant

core output Interpretation of regulations + diagnosis of compliance gaps
Typical applications ✓ What impact does Vietnam Circular 17 have on us?
✓ Which of our processes will BNM inspect this year?
✓ What do we need to upgrade under the new OJK policy?
difference vs Legal Department: AI-accelerated regulatory monitoring + intelligent recommendations

3️⃣ Anti-fraud strategy consultant

core output Rule optimization + new fraud warning
Typical applications ✓ The wave of deep fakes is coming, how to adjust the detection threshold?
✓ How can emerging cases of "twin fraud" be identified in our system?
✓ Fraud risk score by month/region to help us optimize marketing costs?
difference vs traditional rule engine: data-driven AI intelligent suggestions

4️⃣ Investigation assistance

core output Case link + SAR first draft generation
Typical applications ✓ How is this applicant related to known cases of fraud?
✓ Help me automatically organize the evidence and generate a first draft SAR using AML terminology
✓ Pattern detection across branches: Is there an organized fraud ring?
difference vs human investigation: AI deputy rather than replacement for investigators
💡 Product philosophy: ADVANCE.AI’s existing eKYC offering is already strong. The core of this incubation project is not to replace it; Instead, based on the same AI/model, package a Benchmarking professional consulting consultant ability aspect. Users are no longer "system integrators calling APIs" but "business decision-makers using AI consultants". The value of the product is not reflected in the verification accuracy (then we have already done it), but in the Depth of insight, relevance of recommendations, compliance understanding 。

🚨 5 core risks of incubation projects

🔄
SaaS Revenue Cannibalization: Consulting Systems Pricing Conflicts with Existing Contracts
serious
ADVANCE.AI Existing SaaS customers already pay per API call. If the consulting system is launched as an "upgrade package", it will directly affect the existing contract structure.
  • BRI/Mandiri already have a SaaS contract, how to convince them to "add on" rather than "replace"?
  • Gross margins for on-premises versions (60-65%) are lower than SaaS (75-80%), and internal P&L pressure is real
  • If the sales team's commission structure is not adjusted, it may inadvertently lead customers to downgrade rather than upgrade.
Correction: Establish a clear product tiering agreement - SaaS maintains the original API contract, and the consulting system is priced separately as a "value-added consulting layer" (¥30-50K/year software subscription + hardware amortization). Clearly communicate to the sales team: upgrading the advisor system does not reduce API revenue, but adds to it. Completed contract structure trials with 3 customers first.
🏢
Internal Political Resistance: Incubation Projects vs. Mainline SaaS Roadmap
serious
Incubation projects rely on existing sales, engineering, and operations resources, but the priority of these resources is controlled by the SaaS mainline.
  • The sales team's quota is still centered on SaaS ARR, and the priority of the consulting system is naturally low.
  • If engineering resources are "borrowed" by the incubation team, it will directly trigger resistance from the SaaS team
  • If the incubation project Year 1 does not meet expectations, the budget will be cannibalized by the SaaS team in the annual planning
Correction: Incubation projects must obtain an independent headcount (even 4-5 people) and do not "borrow" mainline resources. Establish an independent P&L accounting unit, and decouple KPIs from the SaaS team. It is recommended to report directly to the CEO/CTO rather than through SaaS product line management. At the same time, a "collaboration agreement" is formulated: the incubation team needs to pay internal service fees to use sales resources to quantify their contribution.
🎭
Ambiguous product identity: Customers will confuse "advisor system" with "eKYC API"
serious
The positioning of "identity risk AI advisor" is novel, but the mental model of bank procurement departments is still that of "eKYC software" or "compliance toolbox".
  • The risk control department understands the requirements, but the procurement department classifies them as "IT infrastructure" - leading to the wrong approval process
  • The "consultant" positioning implies manual services. The bank will ask: Is it automatically output by AI, or is there someone behind the scenes?
  • How can the 4 modules (Research/Compliance/Strategy/Investigation) be sold as one coherent product rather than 4 separate plug-ins?
Correction: Create a "Product Definition White Paper" (2-3 pages), making it clear: ① This is an integrated system of software + hardware, without manual intervention; ② The 4 modules are different workflows of the same system, covered by a single contract; ③ The relationship with the existing eKYC API is an "upgrade version" rather than a replacement. Prepare different versions of product briefs for different roles (CRO/CISO/Procurement).
💰
Pricing system conflict: How to upgrade pricing to existing customers without damaging the relationship
medium
Existing SaaS customers are billed on a per-call basis, and the consulting system needs to introduce a hybrid pricing model of hardware amortization + annual software subscription.
  • Customers will compare: the original API call was $0.05, and the new system annual fee is ¥50-80K. Is it worth it?
  • TOC (Total Cost of Ownership) must be clearly calculated, otherwise sales negotiations will become bogged down in price questioning
  • Hardware procurement cycles (bank IT budgets are typically locked in 6-12 months in advance) and incubation timelines may be misaligned
Correction: Design a "migration pricing" plan: the annual fee for the consulting system can be deducted from your existing SaaS contract (up to 30%). Prepare a TOC comparison template to quantify the labor cost savings brought by the advisory system (the annual salary of a risk control analyst is IDR 150M ≈ ¥70K, and the advisory system can replace the workload of 2-3 low-level analysts).
🎯
Restructuring of the competitive landscape: Our real competitor is no longer Jumio
medium
As ADVANCE.AI, competitors have upgraded from "SaaS vendors that can do eKYC" to "players that can copy the consultant positioning".
  • Huawei/Alibaba Cloud has deployed AI risk control solutions in SEA Bank, with localization capabilities
  • Big 4 consulting (Deloitte/KPMG) is marketing AI risk control implementation services to banks, competing directly with the "consultant" positioning
  • After the bank's internal team matures in LLM capabilities, it may choose to build its own "consulting system" instead of outsourcing it.
Correction: The core moat should focus on ADVANCE.AI’s unique assets: ① SEA fraud data accumulated with 3M+ daily requests (Huawei cannot copy); ② Behavior baseline model of existing 500+ customers; ③ BRI/Mandiri’s actual calibration case. Explicitly write these data assets into the product value proposition - "advisor system draws its recommendations from SEA's largest database of real fraud."

Core advantages
Compared with external startups, ADVANCE.AI’s incubation projects have three structural advantages that cannot be replicated:
